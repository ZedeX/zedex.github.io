Being risk is always an option, if being fun.

* * *

You guys know I love Cello. I struggled a long time thinking whether bought a cello or not in SH. I didn't buy it. But here in SZ, I got one! Volla!

If there's one song I would play, I would prefer with no doubt that "Suiten fur Violoncello solo Nr.1 G-dur, BWV.1007" by J.S. Bach (Just Prelude part is fine I think). But I'm sure the first song I'd finish is Kanon D-dur. ![](http://mat1.gtimg.com/www/mb/images/face/29.gif)

By the way, "Orchestral Suite No. 3 In D Major, BWV. 1068/Air" is also a try. But since "Air" is really a hard song and it's originally in violin. I wonder if I could reproduce and finish it in a month. You know, I like fast food.

Money, girls, cello... What a Xanadu! If I somehow could feel blessed, it should be today.
