![NotDelay](/wp-content/uploads/2013/06/NotDelay.jpg)  
Not delay, is an attitude, to the post promises and to the future hopes.

It is a kind of self-confidence, to the telling of macroscopic and to the operation of details.

It is a kind of trust, to the respect of the leaders adn the dependence of the team.

It is an responsibility, to the focus on the cost and the monitoring to the risks...

My love to you, not delay.

不跳票，是一种态度，对过去的承诺，对未来的期许；  
是一种自信，对宏观的预判，对细节的操作；  
是一种信任，对领导的尊重，对团队的依赖；  
是一种责任，对成本的关注，对风险的监控……  
我对你的爱，不跳票。
